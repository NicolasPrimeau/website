Research

Replaces phase 1 of turn

Sequence

  • 1. Buy dice

    Each research dice costs 5 IPC

  • 2. Roll dice

    Roll dice. Each 6 is a success

  • 3. Roll breakthrough dice

    Choose a breaktrough chart and roll a dice for each success

  • 4. Mark developments

    Note the research. Research takes effect immediately

Chart 1

1-Advanced Artillery

One artillery can support 2 infantry or mechanized infantry

2-Rockets

Air bases can launch rockets against enemy structures. Attack of 1, range of 4. Can not be launched over neutral territories.

3-Paratroopers

Up to 2 infatntry units in each territory with an air base can be moved to enemy controlled territory up to 3 spaces away that you are attacking. Can be shot down by AA.

4-Increased Factory Production

Major complexes max increase to 12, minor increased to 4. Repairs costs 1 IPC for 2 repair.

5-War bonds

Roll a die and collect as many IPC during end of turn

6-Improved Mechanized Infantry

Mechanized infantry gain +1 attack when paired with tank. Can now blitz without tank

Chart 2

1-Super Submarines

Subs attack at 3 instead of 2

2-Jet Fighters

Fighters attack at 4. Escorts and Intercepts at 2

3-Improved Shipyards

Sea units are now cheaper:

  • Battleship - 17
  • Aircraft Carrier - 13
  • Cruiser - 9
  • Destroyer - 7
  • Transport - 6
  • Submarine - 5

4-Radar

Fire from structures and AA attacks at 2

5-Long-Range Aircraft

+1 Range for all aircraft

6-Heavy bombers

Roll two dice per strategic bomber and pick the dice you want.
