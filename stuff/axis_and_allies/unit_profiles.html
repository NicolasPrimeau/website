Structures

  • Major Industrial Complex

    Cost: 30

    Attack: -

    Defense: (AA)

    Move: -

    HP: 20"

    -Produces 1 less unit per damage

    -Can produce max 10 units per turn

    -IPC of >=3 on territory

    -Can not be built on island

  • Minor Industrial Complex

    Cost: 12

    Attack: -

    Defense: (AA)

    Move: -

    HP: 6"

    -Can produce max 3 units per turn

    -Can not be destroyed, inoperative after 3 damage

    -Produces 1 less unit per damage

    -Can be upgraded to major with a cost of 20 IPC, if territory already owned that has an IPC of >=3

    -Damaged minor can not be upgraded

    -IPC of >=2 minimum on territory

    -Can not be built on island

    -Inoperative after 3 damage

    -If on island, air units can scramble

  • Naval Base

    Cost: 15

    Attack: -
